Everything shown on this website is made by hand. We ask you to read the following in that spirit.

Handmade variation

Our pieces are embroidered by individual artisans, not by machine. Small differences in colour, motif placement, finish and size are natural and are a sign of genuine hand work, not a fault. Two pieces of the same design will never be exactly alike.

Images and colour

Photographs are intended to show the work as faithfully as possible. The colour and texture you see can vary with your screen, lighting and settings, and the actual piece may look a little different in person.

Prices and availability

This website does not display prices and does not sell online. Prices, sizes and availability are confirmed when you contact us, and may change without notice. As each piece is handmade, an item shown may be sold or may need to be made to order.

Product codes

Some product codes in our catalogue are shared across different ranges — for example our Phulkari and Gota Patti suits. When you enquire, please mention the code together with the collection and technique so we can identify the exact piece.
