Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This website, phpc.in, is published by PESHAS Handicraft Producer Company Ltd. It is an informational catalogue of our hand-embroidered work. We have tried to keep it as simple and respectful of your privacy as the craft it represents.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information through this website. There are no contact forms, no accounts and no login. The site does not set advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not run third-party analytics by default.

If your browser requests our pages, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may process standard technical information such as your IP address and request logs to deliver the site securely. This is handled by the host and is not used by us to identify you.

When you contact us

If you choose to email or call us, you share the details you decide to include — your name, contact details and your enquiry. We use these only to reply to you and to arrange any order you ask for. We do not sell or share them.

Email links on this site open your own email application; nothing is sent until you press send.

External links

Some links lead to other websites — for example the Government of India handicrafts portals and our PDF catalogue. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of websites we do not operate.

Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display the site's typefaces, which may involve a request to Google's servers when a page loads. No personal information is requested for this purpose.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any change will appear on this page with a new date above.

Questions about this page? Write to peshascompany@gmail.com.
